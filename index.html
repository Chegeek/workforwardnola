"I've taken 4 classes with the City so far...I did start at the bottom, but that's ok. I know I had to get there and show people what I'm capable of and show them that my incarceration doesn't lessen my abilities and diminish what I'm capable of."

Rhonda was raised in New Orleans and graduated from Booker T. Washington High School. After serving 20 years in prison, she had a tough time getting a job until a friend told her about STRIVE. Despite the 4 week time commitment she decided to enroll and complete the program. This transformational experience helped her land a job with the City of New Orleans. She recently aced a Microsoft Excel class and is on her way to a promotion and a stable career.
